## 👋 Pack 1: Hello & Goodbye (Greetings)
**🎯 Goal:** How to be polite and friendly!

| Situation | Say This (English) | When Do We Say It? |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Morning** ☀️ | Good morning! 👋 | When the sun is coming up. |
| **Afternoon** 🌤️ | Good afternoon! 👋 | When the sun is high in the sky. |
| **Evening** 🌆 | Good evening! 👋 | When the sun is going down. |
| **Night** 🌙 | Good night! 👋 | When we are sleeping or going to bed. |
| **Meeting someone** | Nice to meet you! / Glad to meet you. 😊 | When you meet a new friend. |

## 🔊 Pack 4: Sounds and Seasons (Fun Fact!)
**🎯 Goal:** Learning sounds and about the weather.

### 🎶 Fun with Sounds (Phonics!)
We use these letter sounds to read and write words!

*   **B-A-T:** Bat, Bag, Mat (Things that start with B and have -AT)
*   **C-A-P:** Cap, Map, Tap (Things that start with C and have -AP)
*   **F-A-N:** Fan, Ran, Man (Things that have the short 'a' sound)

### 🌧️ All About Seasons and Rain
*   **Rainy Season:** ☔💧 (The cloud cries, but the plant grows!)
*   **Summer:** 😎☀️ (Hot and sunny! Swimming!)
*   **Winter:** 🧣🌬️ (Cold! Sweaters and warmth.)

**📖 Story Summary: We Want Rain!**
The story was about the three friends who needed rain:
1.  The Peacock 🕺 (Wants to dance!)
2.  The Fish 🐟 (Wants more water!)
3.  The Farmer 🌱 (Wants to plant seeds!)
**Result:** The cloud rained, and everyone was happy! 🎉

# 🚀 Your Quick Revision Checklist (Before Bed!)
Use this list every day to make sure you remember everything!

✅ **Say Good Morning** (Even if it's pretend!)
✅ **Name 3 Body Parts** (Touch them while you say the word.)
✅ **Name 3 Actions** (Can you swim? Can you read?)
✅ **Say Good Night** to your toys or teddy bear.
✅ **Look around** and try to say the name of one thing you see (e.g., "chair," "book").
