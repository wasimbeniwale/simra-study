Hello, little learner! This unit is like an adventure. We learned about healthy foods, our bodies, animals, and even a funny story! Let's review everything we learned.

### 🌱 Topic 1: Veggies and Healthy Eating (Pages 20-21)
*   **What we learned:** Many different vegetables like carrots, spinach, pumpkins, and beans. They are very good for us!
*   **New Words:** Carrot, Spinach, Pumpkin, Cabbage, Peas.
*   **Activity Practice:** We practiced asking: "Do you like spinach?" and answering: "Yes, I do," or "No, I don't."

### 👋 Topic 2: Our Body and Playtime (Pages 21-23)
*   **Body Parts:** We learned about our fingers and hands (Thumb, Index finger, Middle finger, Wrist, Palm).
*   **Action Words:** We sang songs about our fingers dancing and helped us know how to climb a hill!
*   **Playground Fun:** We learned about places we play: Slide, Swing, Seesaw, Bench, Sandpit.

### 🐾 Topic 3: Animal Friends (Pages 24-25)
*   **Farm Animals:** Cow, Goat, Buffalo (our pets!).
*   **Wild Animals:** Lion, Tiger, Bear, Wolf, Elephant (their homes are den/home).
*   **Action:** We learned that animals live in different places (like a tree or a cage).

### 🗣️ Topic 4: Sounds and Songs (Pages 26-33)
*   **Phonics Fun:** We practiced sounds like **h**, **l**, **r**, **s**, and then **g**, **j**, **k**, **q**. We matched these sounds to words (e.g., sun, soap, lamp).
*   **Action Rhymes:** We sang about animals that do things: Birds can fly, Frogs can jump, Dogs can walk.
*   **Birthday Party:** We learned new words for a party: Gift, Cake/Candle, Crown, Balloons.

### 📖 Topic 5: The Story of the Monkey (Pages 34-37)
*   **The Moral:** We heard a story about two cats and a monkey who shared butter. The monkey taught us that **sharing is important** and we must divide things equally!
*   **New Skill:** We practiced telling stories with pictures.
