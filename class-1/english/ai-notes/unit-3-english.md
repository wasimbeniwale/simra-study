Hello Sweetie! Let's learn about all the cool things we read in Unit 3. These notes will help you remember everything!

### 🚦 Topic 1: Traffic Signals & Colors (Page 38-40)
*   **Traffic Light Meanings:**
    *   🟢 **Green:** Go! (Meaning: Move forward.)
    *   🟡 **Yellow:** Wait! (Meaning: Be ready to stop or go soon.)
    *   🔴 **Red:** Stop! (Meaning: Halt right now!)
*   **Rainbow Colors (VIBGYOR):** Remember the colors in the rainbow! Violet, Indigo, Blue, Green, Yellow, Orange, Red.
*   **My Favorite Colors:** Match the colors you see in the picture (Red, Yellow, Green, Blue, etc.).

### 🚀 Topic 2: Vehicles & Journeys (Page 41-43)
*   **Kinds of Transport:** We use many ways to travel! Examples: Car, Bus, Train, Aeroplane, Boat, Bicycle.
*   **Travel Dialogue:** When we go on a journey, we ask: "Where are you going?" and "Who is with you?"
*   **Saying Goodbye:** We use phrases like, "Happy Journey," and "Goodbye."

### 🍽️ Topic 3: Being Helpful at Lunch Time (Page 44)
*   **Classroom Instructions:** These are important rules!
    1.  Make a queue (Stand in line).
    2.  Wash your hands properly.
    3.  Sit in a row.
    4.  Don’t waste food (Be thankful for your meal!).
    5.  Clean up afterward!

### 🅰️ Topic 4: The Alphabet Adventure (Pages 38-40, 45-47)
*   **A to Z:** We sing the Alphabet Song! Every letter has a sound (like A says 'ant').
*   **Sounds:** We practice the sounds for V, W, X, Y, Z (like *zip*, *zoo*, *xylophone*).
*   **Little Words:** We learn small words like **is, am, are, was, were**.
*   **Big Letters:** We learn how to write big letters (Capital) and small letters.

### 🔢 Topic 5: Numbers, Shapes & Time (Pages 49-53)
*   **Counting:** We count from Zero (0) to Ten (10). 🥇 **Zero is important!**
*   **Shapes:** Every object has a shape!
    *   ⚫ **Circle:** Round, like a ball.
    *   🔺 **Triangle:** Three sides (like a small roof).
    *   ⬛ **Square:** Four equal sides.
    *   📌 **Dot/Line:** Small points and long lines.
*   **The Week:** We know that there are 7 days in a week! (Monday, Tuesday... Sunday).
*   **Time Words:** We use these words to talk about time: *Yesterday*, *Today*, and *Tomorrow*.

### 🐜 Topic 6: Story Time – The Ant and the Pigeon (Pages 54-58)
*   **The Moral Story:** An Ant fell in the pond. A kind Pigeon helped it by throwing a leaf. Later, the Ant saved the Pigeon from the hunter!
*   **The Lesson:** Always help others. Good deeds come back to you! (Kindness is important.)
