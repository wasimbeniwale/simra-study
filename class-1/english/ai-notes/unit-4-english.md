### ⭐ Our Calendar & Time (Months)
*   **A Year:** Has 12 months. They are like a big journey!
*   **The Months Are:** January, February, March, April, May, June, July, August, September, October, November, and December.
*   **Activity:** We learned to complete the names of the months!

### 👋 Saying Hello & Goodbye (Greetings)
*   **Be Kind:** Always use nice words!
    *   Happy Birthday! 🎂
    *   Thank you! 🙏
    *   Merry Christmas / Happy Diwali / Eid-Mubarak (Different festival greetings).
    *   Congratulations! 🎉 (When someone does something great).
    *   All the Best / Happy Journey! (Wishing luck).

### 🚿 My Daily Routines
*   **After My Bath:** We sing a song to remember parts of our body: Nose, toes, hands, fingers, and two wet legs!
*   **Bathroom Things:** Tap, towel, shampoo, soap, toothbrushes. Keep things tidy!
*   **Lost and Found:** If you lose your whistle or bottle, don't worry! Ask politely. "Hi, Sparrow! Is this yours?"

### 🥣 My Kitchen Adventures
*   **Kitchen Tools:** We use many tools: Pot, pan, cooker, ladle, measuring jug.
*   **Making Sharbat:** We followed a recipe! (Water + Lemon Juice + Sugar + Salt = Sweet Sharbat).
*   **Action Words:** Stir, serve, pour, take, add.

### 🏘️ Our Community Heroes (Helpers)
*   **Different Jobs:** Every job is important!
    *   **Doctor:** Helps when we are sick. 🤒
    *   **Teacher:** Helps us learn ABCs and numbers. 🍎
    *   **Soldier:** Keeps our country safe on the border. 🛡️
    *   **Police:** Keep things safe in our town/city. 🚨
    *   **Driver:** Drives the bus and takes people places.

### ✨ Wonderful Stories & Gratitude
*   **The Magic Fish:** The fish taught us that colors make life beautiful and happy! 🌈
